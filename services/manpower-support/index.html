Manpower support services & training

"Because we are Dark Horse, driven by an abiding quest for excellence, we try harder.....every time"

Recruitment

  • We assist our clients in assessing their technical and operational manpower needs, develop and align the staff profile with the job function
  • Using a combination of headhunting and adverting for the position, we source, screen, conduct aptitude & psychometric tests and interviews.
  • We then assist with the engagement and onboarding of successful candidates.

Payroll management

  • We assist our clients in managing the payroll function for supporting staff.
  • We ensure that payroll is properly estimated and paid at the agreed date, while ensuring that all statutory deductions are accurately deducted and remitted.

Training

  • We assist clients with training their support staff, using curriculum specifically developed for the company
