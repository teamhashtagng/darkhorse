About Us

Dark Horse Expeditors ("DHE") was born out of the need to support businesses in enhancing their value creation potential and performance.

Your business can enhance sustainable value creation by focusing on core operations, while outsourcing critical but non-core business support functions and activities. This is where DHE comes in.

Our goal is to provide you with business support solutions for operations and logistics intensive businesses, allowing you to focus on core operations.
